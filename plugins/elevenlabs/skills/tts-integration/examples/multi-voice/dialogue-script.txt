# Sample Dialogue Script for Multi-Speaker TTS
# Format: A: Speaker A text
#         B: Speaker B text

A: Hello! It's great to finally meet you in person.
B: Likewise! I've heard so many good things about your work.
A: That's very kind of you to say. I'm excited about this collaboration.
B: Me too! I think we're going to create something amazing together.
A: Absolutely. So, where should we start?
B: Well, I've been thinking about the initial framework. Let me share my ideas.
A: I'm all ears. Please go ahead.
B: The key is to focus on user experience from the very beginning.
A: I completely agree. That should be our north star.
B: Exactly! And with your expertise in design, I think we can really nail this.
A: I appreciate your confidence. Let's schedule our next meeting to dive deeper.
B: Sounds perfect. How about next Tuesday at ten?
A: Tuesday at ten works great for me. I'll prepare some mockups.
B: Excellent! I'll bring the technical specifications we discussed.
A: Perfect. I'm looking forward to it!
B: Me too. This is going to be an exciting project!
